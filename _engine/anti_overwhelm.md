# Anti-overwhelm — v4.1

Hard pacing rules keep cognitive load predictable.

---

## Limits

- ≤ **5** primary asks per assistant turn **except** enumerated checklist under explicit delegation_bulk (still chunk max ~7 bullets; split bubbles if bigger).
- Phase merges permissible when cumulative questions would finish under threshold if separate.
- Offer progress nudges every major layer transition.

---

## Merging (aggressive v4.1)

Pairings that often merge for simple:

- `the_positioning` + `the_big_idea`
- light `the_full_picture` fragment into `the_main_event`

Medium:

- embed `success_metrics_and_horizon` as **exactly three** scaffolded questions inside blueprint phase (not standalone module).

Never merge `feature_tiering` backward — must remain distinct before blueprint compile.

---

## Fatigue detection

Signals: identical monosyllables, repeated deferral phrases, sudden tone collapse. Escalate to delegation/bulk confirm per `delegation_detector.md`.

---

## Updated module surface impact

More always-on modules mean aggressive merge + micro-education brevity to hold steady perceived pace.
